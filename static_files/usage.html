Usage:
 1. Click on Subscribe on the left navigation bar to subscribe/unsubscribe
   to a plugin's alerts.
   To subscribe to a plugin alert:
    - click on the radio button beside your email and then check the
      plugins you are interested in
    - click on subscribe button
   To unsubscribe to a plugin alert:
    - uncheck the plugin corresponding to your email
    - click on subscribe button
 2. Similarly click on Users to manage users.
 3. And click on Plugins to list all plugins

CLI:
 List of all plugins:
  curl http://localhost:8080/plugin\?plugin\=all\&type\=json
 List of all users:
  curl http://localhost:8080/plugin\?user\=all\&type\=json
 List of plugins subscribed by an email:
  curl http://localhost:8080/plugin\?user\=YOUR EMAIL ID\&type\=json
 List of users subscribed to a plugin:
  curl http://localhost:8080/plugin\?plugin\=PLUGIN\&type\=json
 To subscribe to plugins:
  curl -X POST -d "user=YOUR EMAIL ID" -d "plugin=mysl,ngx" localhost:8080/plugin
